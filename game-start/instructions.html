How to Play Hangman

Welcome to the classic word-guessing game! Here's how you play:

Objective

Your goal is to guess the hidden word, one letter at a time, before the hangman is fully drawn.

Rules

  • A word will be chosen randomly and displayed as a series of blank spaces representing each letter in the word.
  • Guess a letter by clicking or typing it. If the letter is correct, it will appear in the correct position(s).
  • If the guessed letter is incorrect, a part of the hangman will be drawn.
  • You have a limited number of incorrect guesses (e.g., 6 chances) before the game ends.
  • If you guess the entire word correctly before running out of guesses, you win!

Tips

  • Start with vowels (A, E, I, O, U) as they are commonly used in words.
  • Pay attention to the length of the word and guess letters that fit common patterns.
  • If you're unsure, guess letters that are frequently used in English, such as T, N, S, or R.

Winning and Losing

  • Win: Reveal all the letters of the word before the hangman is complete.
  • Lose: The hangman is fully drawn before you guess the word.
